Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Short version: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The game runs entirely in your browser. No accounts, no tracking, no cookies, no analytics. Your game data stays on your device only.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how this Site ("GTA Vice City Online") handles information when you visit and use the service. We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect any personal information.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, process, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Device information or browser fingerprints
  • Usage data, session data, or behavioural analytics
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers of any kind
  • Account credentials (no accounts are required or supported)

3. Local Storage (OPFS)

When you use the Site for the first time, the game files (~701 MB) are downloaded and stored in your browser's Origin Private File System (OPFS) — a sandboxed local storage area managed entirely by your browser.

  • This data is stored only on your device
  • It is never transmitted to any server, including ours
  • It is accessible only to this Site within your browser
  • You can delete it at any time using the "Reset game data" button on the main page, or by clearing your browser's site data

4. Service Workers

The Site uses a Service Worker to intercept requests for game assets and serve them from your local OPFS storage. This is a browser-native offline technology. The Service Worker does not communicate with any external server or transmit any data.

5. Third-Party Services

During the one-time game setup, files are downloaded from the Internet Archive (archive.org). Your request to that service is subject to the Internet Archive's own Terms of Use and Privacy Policy. We do not control or have access to any data those services may collect.

We do not embed any third-party analytics, advertising networks, social media trackers, or CDN scripts on this Site.

6. Cookies

This Site does not use cookies of any kind — not for session management, not for analytics, not for advertising. There is no cookie banner because there are no cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

This Site does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). Because we collect no personal data from anyone, this applies equally to all users.

8. Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. All game data stored on your device is fully within your control. You can remove it at any time through the "Reset game data" button or your browser's settings.

9. Hosting & Infrastructure

This Site is hosted on Netlify. Netlify may log standard web server access logs (such as IP addresses and request paths) for security and operational purposes. These logs are managed by Netlify under their own Privacy Policy. We do not have access to or use these logs for any tracking purpose.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you may open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
