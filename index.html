How to Play

  1. Open the page — the game archive downloads automatically in the background (first visit only).
  2. Watch the progress bar — download is ~701 MB and may take a few minutes.
  3. Click Start to Play once the bar reaches 100%.
  4. Future visits skip the download and load instantly from local storage.

Best on Chrome or Firefox desktop. Also works on mobile browsers.

Features

  • 🌐Runs entirely in the browser — no plugins or downloads
  • 💾Game stored locally via OPFS — loads instantly after first visit
  • 📴Works offline after installation
  • 🎮Gamepad support on desktop
  • 📱Touch controls on mobile
  • ⚡Powered by WebAssembly for near-native performance
